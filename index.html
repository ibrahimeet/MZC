ℹ️ About

Purpose

This calculator helps Muslims in India compute their annual Zakat obligation — a pillar of Islam requiring 2.5% of eligible wealth to be given to those in need.

Features

  • Live Gold & Silver prices from Metal Price API
  • INR-based calculations for Indian Muslims
  • Silver Nisab (recommended by scholars)
  • Shariah-compliant methodology
  • PDF & CSV export for records
  • Feedback system for continuous improvement

Disclaimer

This is a guide only. For complex situations (business Zakat, inherited wealth, etc.), consult a qualified Islamic scholar familiar with your madhab.

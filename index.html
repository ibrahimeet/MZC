💡 Understanding Nisab

Nisab is the minimum threshold of wealth one must possess before Zakat becomes obligatory.

Gold Nisab: 87.48 grams (≈ 7.5 tola)
Silver Nisab: 612.36 grams (≈ 52.5 tola)

Most contemporary scholars recommend using the Silver Nisab as it is lower and ensures more Muslims fulfil their Zakat obligation.

📊 Historical Context

These thresholds come from the time of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ when 200 dirhams (silver) or 20 dinars (gold) represented significant wealth. Today's value fluctuates with market prices, which is why we provide live updates.
